About "Inspirational Thoughts"

"Inspirational Thoughts" is an app that lets you explore, browse, and search the great words of wisdom (quotes & thoughts) of some greatest personalities of all time. Here in the selected simple but incredible thoughts/advices, you can easily find answers to various problems. For deep thinkers, these also serve as "Food-for-Thoughts".

Abraham Lincoln :- 16th President of United States.He preserved the Union,abolished slavery and modernized the economy.
Albert Einstein :- He was the theoretical physicist.He developed the general theory of relativity and best known for his formula:- E = mc2
APJ Abdul Kalam :- He was the 11th President of India.He was a Professor, Author, Aerospace Scientist and known as the missile man of India.
Bill Gates :- He is an American Business magnate, philanthropist, investor, computer programmer and inventor.He is co-founder of Microsoft which is world largest software company.
Charles Darwin :- He was an English naturalist and geologist, best known for his contributions to evolutionary theory.
Gautama Buddha :- The founder of Buddhism.He was the another form of lord Vishnu.
John F. Kennedy :- He was an American politician and 35th President of the United States.
Mahatma Gandhi :- He was father of nation of India.He was the preeminent leader of Indian independence movement.
Mother Teresa :- She was a Roman Catholic religious sister and missionary.She founded the Missionary of Charity, a Roman Catholic religious congregation.
Nelson Mandela :- He was 1st President of South Africa.He was an South Africa Anti-apartheid revolutionary, politician and philanthropist.
Steve Jobs :- He was an American businessman and best known as the co-founder, chairman and CEO of Apple Inc.Also known for Pioneer of the personal computer revolution.
Swami Vivekanand :- He was an Indian Hindu monk and founder of Ramakrishna Mission and Ramakrishna Math.He was introduced the Indian philosophies of Vedanta and Yoga to the Western world.
Winston Churchill :- He was the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom.He was a British statesman, Member of Parliament, solider, journalist, historian, author and painter.


Note:"Inspirational Thoughts" is an unofficial, fan-made, open source application, and the contents are taken from web sources like Wikipedia, Encyclopedia, Blogspot etc.
